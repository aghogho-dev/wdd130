Overview

Purpose

We are a professional guides dedicated to leading trip down the most beautiful rivers in the Niger Delta. Our most popular trips in River Ethiope and Aragba are open all year round. We also offer a few special trips in other parts of the Niger Delta during the holiday season.

Audience

We carter to adventurers, especially people who enjoy exploring rivers and natural water bodies. Most of our visitors are between the ages of 15 and 50 years old. What do our visitors want? They want a thrilling experience that get their adrenaline pumping and they love being in and around water. What do our visitors expect to find on our website? They will find information abour our different rafting trips planned for the year and easy way to book them.
